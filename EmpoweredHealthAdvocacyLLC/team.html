Meet Your Advocacy Team

Nicki Meyer

RN, MSN

The passion for bridging the gap... between patients and care providers was sparked by a series of health events in Nicki's young adulthood, which gave her first-hand experience with frustrating connection gaps with her care providers. Her 12-years of experience nursing ranges from emergency rooms in Missouri & Texas, helicopter primary response, high-risk labor & delivery, maternal transport, and COVID-19 home visits. The pandemic has emphasized the need for health advocates everywhere, so while living in Union, Missouri with her husband and two teenage boys, Nicki is committed to being the health advocate her community needs.

Jessie Fleming

RN, BSN, C-EFM

The passion for helping others... through nursing was embedded in Jessie after having her son at an early age. As a 2017 graduate of the nursing program at Maryville University, she jumped right into working high-risk labor and delivery and had since stayed. Although, seeing the lack of patient support and navigating through healthcare, has brought her to advocate full-time for patients in the Gerald, Missouri community where she calls home with her husband and their two children.
