Our Services

Health Advocates

A healthcare advocate helps you navigate the complex world of healthcare by providing education on hospital policies, patient rights, present options that may be available to you, and assists with preparing questions and concerns to effectively communicate to your doctor.

Doulas

A doula is a trained and experienced medical professional who provides continuous physical, emotional, and educational support to a birthing person (and their partner, if present) before, during, and just after delivery. A doula can help with many things including birth plan creation, locating needed resources, and providing labor support including helping patients advocate their desires.
